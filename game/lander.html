>_ Co-op Lander
One ship, two pilots: one fires the main engine (thrust), the other steers the tilt. Tilt to aim the nose, then thrust to push that way — and to slow your fall. Work together to set it down softly and upright on the pad. Fast, off-pad, or upside-down = crash. Play remotely (Create/Join) or on one device.

// controls
Thrust: W / ↑ / Space (or hold ▲).
Tilt: ← / → or A / D — or slide the joystick: the further you slide, the faster you turn.
Tip: tilt aims the ship; thrust pushes wherever the nose points. Release tilt and it stops turning.
Local: one of you on thrust, one on tilt — same keyboard.
