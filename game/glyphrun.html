A tiny lesson in disguise: the script eras (楷書 → 篆書 → 金文 → 甲骨文) are real, and so are the character families — 大 is a person with arms spread, 舞 a dancer with plumes, 牢 an ox in its pen, 犇 a stampede, 美 a dancer crowned with ram horns, 羴 a whole flock, and 為 is a hand leading an elephant to work. Han Fei wrote that people who had never seen a living elephant imagined one from its bones — 想象, “to imagine”, is literally picturing the elephant.
